2 ### Work with the console
3 
4 With the console page you can monitor the activities of your system and send your
5 own commands to the SIM808 module.
6 
7 You can set the level of detail using the **Debug Active** option.
8 
9 Note: To communicate with the SIM808 module this must be switched on
10 
11  ![Console Page](../images/Console.png "Console Page")
12 
13 If you want to send your own AT commands to the SIM808 it is advisable to deactivate the
14 SMS, GPS and MQTT functions under Settings during this time, otherwise they may interfere
15 with your commands.
16
